Physics

As a student who pursued a degree in Physics at Universidad de Guadalajara, my academic journey has led me into the fascinating realm of semiconductors. In particular, my thesis centres around the processes of thin film deposition using Pulsed Laser Deposition. By employing this technique, I explore the synthesis and characterisation of semiconductor thin films. The focus lies in understanding the growth mechanisms, optimising deposition parameters, and evaluating the structural, optical, and electrical properties of the deposited films. My research aims to contribute to the development of semiconductor technology. The findings obtained from this study will enhance our understanding of the fundamental processes involved in thin film growth and provide insights into the development of high-performance semiconductor devices. It is my fervent hope that my thesis will contribute to practical applications in various fields such as electronics, photonics, and energy conversion.

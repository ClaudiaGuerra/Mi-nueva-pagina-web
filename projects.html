Projects

I've stepped into the exciting world of semiconductors. My thesis focuses on thin film deposition using Pulsed Laser Deposition, a technique that helps us create and study semiconductor thin films. I investigate how these films grow, adjust the deposition process, and analyse their structure, optical, and electrical properties. My goal is to boost semiconductor technology by gaining a deeper understanding of how these films form and work. I hope my research will lead to advancements in electronics, photonics, and energy conversion, making a real impact in these fields. I've been working on different projects such as "Silicon Carbide thin films deposited using laser ablation at different fluence" and "Influence of Nitrogen on the optical and structural properties of Silicon Carbide thin films deposited by PLD".

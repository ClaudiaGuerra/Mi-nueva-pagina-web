Physics

As a Physics student at Universidad de Guadalajara, I've stepped into the exciting world of semiconductors. My thesis focuses on thin film deposition using Pulsed Laser Deposition, a technique that helps us create and study semiconductor thin films. I investigate how these films grow, adjust the deposition process, and analyse their structure, optical, and electrical properties. My goal is to boost semiconductor technology by gaining a deeper understanding of how these films form and work. I hope my research will lead to advancements in electronics, photonics, and energy conversion, making a real impact in these fields.
